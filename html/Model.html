Model 2020


GATEE01 is the first ever go-kart model designed entirely from scratch by our design team.Various design innovations are included in this model in great depths. The major innovation of GATEE01 is the engine cooling side pods. Several FEA procedures were also performed on this particular model yielding satisfactory results and attempts towards an exactly replicated final model are being made.
