The challenge to teams is to develop a kart (CV and EV) that can successfully compete in all the events described in the GKDC Rulebook. IKRC


IKRC (Indian Kart Racing Championship) is a Go-Kart competition in India which provides a platform for young Engineers to design their own motor vehicle to compete. IKRC challenges teams of university undergraduate and graduate students to conceive, design, fabricate, develop and compete with Karts that are solely developed by student teams. To give teams the maximum design flexibility and the freedom to express their creativity and imaginations there are very few restrictions on the overall vehicle design. We were acclaimed with an overall AIR 31 in this contest !
